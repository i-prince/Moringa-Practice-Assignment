Fried Chicken with Chips

Definition

Chips is a type of potatoes that go hand in hand with fried chicken.

How to prepare Chips

  1. Start by peeling the potatoe skin
  2. Thinly chop the potatoes into small pieces
  3. Heat oil on your cooker
  4. Deep your potatoes in the hot oil, once they are ready to your liking remove
The chips will be served with the fried chicken we prepared.
